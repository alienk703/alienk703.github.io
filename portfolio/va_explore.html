Plan now for the coming academic year with VA education and training programs. These benefits aren’t just for college, though—you can use them to train for just about any career you can imagine. Watch this video to explore the possibilities.

The following VA programs can help cover the cost of tuition, housing, training, and more:

  • The Post-9/11 GI Bill® offers higher education and training benefits to Veterans and Servicemembers who served after Sept. 10, 2001.
  • If you are heading to college, VA’s Yellow Ribbon Program may make additional funds available beyond the GI Bill entitlement.
  • The Montgomery GI Bill and other proframs may be available to Veterans from previous service eras.
  • Survivors’ and Dependents’ Educational Assistance provides opportunities for certain Veterans’ spouses, dependents, and survivors too.
