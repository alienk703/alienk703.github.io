Gain early access to The Nation’s Report Card: 2014 U.S. History, Geography, and Civics

On April 29, 2015, the National Assessment Governing Board will release The Nation’s Report Card: 2014 U.S. History, Geography, and Civics. These results from the National Assessment of Educational Progress will provide insight into students’ knowledge of our nation’s past, global geography, and the fundamentals of democratic government.

We are sharing the results on an embargoed basis before the public release to assist your office in handling inquiries from media or the public.

Embargoed Materials

To access The Nation’s Report Card: 2014 U.S. History, Geography, and Civics and other embargoed materials, please visit https://www.nationsreportcard.org and enter your username and password.
