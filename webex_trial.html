Cisco Webex 30 day Trial Process

Brief

Not all of Cisco partners wants to use the custom webex website. Some of them wants to keep it in their own site, and for them, we have created a trial process for these partners. They can simply add a button on their site, which will lead the user to free 30-day Webex Trial sign up process.

Created at:

Cisco Systems Collaboration Technology Group:

Project

This brandable trial process is to provide 30-day free Webex Trial experience for businesses who visit Cisco and Cisco partners. The user experience was kept to minimal steps, with option of adding additional team members, and try-out devices.

Partner Customization

Branding is important. Engagement Center websites provide cutomization experience for the Partners so they can include their logo, font and their color palette

User Flow

Within 3 simple steps, the user can add their admin email account, up to 4 additional team members, order devices for the trial, and check out.
