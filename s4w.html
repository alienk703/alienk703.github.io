S4W (Sports 4 Win) Web Design and Branding

I wanted to create a sports news website where everyone can have their opinions heard. When I read the comments on other sports sites, everyone seems to be an expert so lets hear them. In S4W, users can vote for the MVP of each game and Wasted Effort (best performer on losing team). Each players get points and users can see which players get the most votes through out the season. Also on news articles, users can vote on who won the deal, trades, and so on.
